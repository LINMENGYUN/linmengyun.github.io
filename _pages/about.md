I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Xiamen University. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the National University of Singapore in 2022. My research lies at the intersection of health economics, applied microeconomics, applied econometrics, and behavioral economics.

My recent work studies topics including health insurance design, population aging, food choice, maternal education, and the effects of social policy on health and economic outcomes. My research has appeared in or is forthcoming in journals including the *Journal of Public Economics*, *JAMA Health Forum*, *Education Economics*, *China Economic Review*, and *Social Science & Medicine*.
